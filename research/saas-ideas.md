## Aplicații B2C Validate cu Revenue Real

### 1. PDF Tools (iLovePDF/Smallpdf) ⭐ ALES PENTRU IMPLEMENTARE
- **Revenue:** Smallpdf $8-11M/an, iLovePDF $1.4M/an
- **Users:** 30M users/lună pe Smallpdf
- **Complexitate:** Ușoară
- **Features MVP:** Merge PDF, Split PDF, Compress PDF, PDF to Image
- **Monetizare:** Freemium - 3 files free/zi, apoi $5-10/lună
- **Tech:** PDF.js (browser), pdf-lib, PyPDF2

### 2. AI Headshot Generator (HeadshotPro)
- **Revenue:** $300k/lună ($3.6M ARR)
- **Fondator:** Danny Postma (solo)
- **Complexitate:** Medie (necesită AI/ML)
- **Features MVP:** Upload selfies → AI generează headshots profesionale
- **Monetizare:** $29-99 per set de headshots
- **Tech:** Stable Diffusion, DreamBooth, Replicate API
- **Notă:** Piață saturată, dar încă profitabilă

### 3. QR Code Generator (Flowcode)
- **Revenue:** Flowcode $15.7M/an, Trycon/Scanova $7.2M/an, TAGO ~$1M/an
- **Complexitate:** Foarte ușoară
- **Features MVP:** QR static, QR dinamic cu tracking, customizare culori/logo
- **Monetizare:** Free basic, $5-15/lună pentru QR dinamice + analytics
- **Tech:** qrcode libraries, foarte simplu

### 4. Background Remover (remove.bg)
- **Revenue:** Estimat $10M+/an
- **Complexitate:** Medie (AI API)
- **Features MVP:** Upload imagine → remove background → download
- **Monetizare:** 1-2 free/zi, apoi $0.20-1/imagine sau $10/lună unlimited
- **Tech:** remove.bg API, Replicate, sau self-hosted AI models

### 5. Landing Page Builder (Carrd)
- **Revenue:** $1.5-2M ARR
- **Fondator:** AJ (solo dev)
- **Complexitate:** Medie-Mare
- **Features MVP:** Drag-and-drop builder, templates, custom domains
- **Monetizare:** $9-49/an
- **Tech:** React/Vue, drag-and-drop libraries
